About Us

We are a Team of passionate professionals that live and breath Workplace Safety everyday. Our ultimate goal each day is to provide a safe work environment for every worker and to return each employee home and uninjured. PCS Safety Inc “PCS” partners with like-minded businesses that share a similar passion and who seek to take Work Safety to the next level. Our proven service delivery is customized for everyone client as we understand that each business is unique in service and culture.

PCS adopts principles of Occupational Health and Safety assessment systems (OSHAS) 18001. This system includes but not limited to assessing, planning, implementation, training, evaluation and regular review of Health and Safety Programs including the effectiveness of each program.

Our Clients

As a certified and versatile firm, we pride ourselves in the ability to serve in multiple regions. Most often, our clients are in the Government, Manufacturing, Transportation, Automotive, Health-Care, Restaurant, Construction and Insurance industries (just to mention a few).

Through our proprietary technology and Professional staff, we are able to deliver services and products that bring immediate value and return on investment. Client’s contract PCS Safety because of our work ethic and the fact that our clients can count on us to ‘Move their Business Ahead’.’

Generally, our clients are high growth companies that are looking for OSHA Compliance Training or Written Safety Compliance Programs. Frequently clients will contact us because they received a notice from OSHA regarding a workers’ compensation high experience modification (ex-mod notice) or a work-related injury occurred and the client is seeking assistance with responding to OSHA, developing a Safety Compliance Program or simply does not have the resources to handle OSHA matters. No matter what your need is we can most likely navigate you through the complexity of Cal OSHA or OSHA related compliance.
